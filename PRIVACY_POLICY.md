# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: Tuesday, 24 March 2026**

ReVoice-Bot ("the Bot") values your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide voice channel management. This includes:
- **Discord IDs:** User IDs, Server (Guild) IDs, Channel IDs, and Role IDs required for permissions.
- **Voice State Metadata:** We monitor connection events (joining/leaving/moving) to trigger channel creation. **Important: We do not—and cannot—listen to, record, or store any voice audio or video data.**
- **Command Logs:** Metadata about command usage to help with performance monitoring and debugging.

## 2. Data Usage
Collected data is used solely for:
- Providing the Bot's core functionality.
- Persisting user and server settings.
- Troubleshooting and improving the service.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell or trade your data. Data may be shared with third parties only if required by law or necessary for the Bot's operation (e.g., hosting providers), subject to their own privacy policies.

## 4. Data Retention
- **Active Data:** Configuration data is stored as long as the Bot remains in your server.
- **Automatic Deletion:** If the Bot is kicked or the server is deleted, associated configuration data is purged from our database within 30 days.
- **Manual Deletion:** Server owners may request immediate data removal via the [Support Server](https://discord.gg/SPfjT5SJ4Q).

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to request the deletion of your data.
- To request data deletion, please contact us or remove the Bot from your server.
- Removing the Bot from a server may result in the deletion of associated configuration data.

## 6. Changes to Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be announced via our [Support Server](https://discord.gg/SPfjT5SJ4Q).

## 7. Contact
For privacy concerns, please contact the developer via our [Support Server](https://discord.gg/SPfjT5SJ4Q).
